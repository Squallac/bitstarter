Welcome to the MSM project...

A cloud-based app for Multi-level marketing entrepreneurs or direct sellers our App is specially designed to grow and manage easily your business tasks. The The friendly User Interface of MSM makes possible the use of the app for all kind of people. An equilibrated combination of simplicity and power... Of course with a touch of elegance.

Features

Manage Contacts

Manage your contacts easily with this feature. You can filter your contacts by clients or prospects, you can Add new contacts manually or import it from your cellphone or facebook.

In this section you can call your contact, send an email, open his facebook profile, add notes with attached documents (pdf, ptt, docx) or track his address with the Google GPS.

Motivational Daily Quotes

You need some motivational inspiration to achieve your goals? Don't worry! At the center of the home screen you can found a business motivational quote.

The quotes are carefully selected for entrepeneurs who are willing to grow their business. If you need to inspire someone else, you can share your quote in the social networks.

"In our survey made to MLM entrepreneurs, 85% of the people found this App very useful!"-Raul Ruiz R. CEO of MSM

Manage Inventory

Download the most recent inventory from your favorite MLM company or Import your own in a CSV file! You can View, edit or Add products to your clients orders for a faster and better management of your products.

If maths are a headache to you... Don't worry! The App calculates your total earnings automatically.

"Globally the MLM industry grew by 5.4% last year; and nearly 14% since 2010. The nearly 14% growth in mlm sales since 2010 is promising when you consider that we were in a global recession during that time" - WDSFA

Network Marketing Startup Guide

If you are new in the business of the 21 century You'll need this guide. You're going to learn what tools you will need, what actions you will need to take, how to schedule your time, and how to lay a remarkable foundation for your business by creating duplication right away.

Share this information with your new prospects to help them with the launch of their business. As a Bonus, there is a test for your MLM prospects where you can see if someone is interested on your business or simply not!
